  You can never have too many [[Chase Ultimate Rewards]] points. This transferable rewards currency gives you access to some of the best hotel and airline transfer partners; therefore, it remains one of the most valuable to have in your wallet. According to TPG's May 2026 valuations, Ultimate Rewards points are worth 2.05 cents each, which is what you should aim for when redeeming your points to help you maximize your rewards. However, you can also use your points to book car rentals, hotels, and flights in the easy-to-use Chase Travel℠ portal when using certain Chase credit cards. Generally, however, transferring to partners like World of Hyatt and Virgin Atlantic Flying Club provides the most value.

## Key Transfer Partners
- **Hotels**: [[World of Hyatt]] is highlighted as the strongest hotel transfer partner (fixed award charts, outsized value in categories 1-5).
- **Airlines**: [[Virgin Atlantic Flying Club]], [[United MileagePlus]], [[Air France-KLM Flying Blue]], and [[Singapore KrisFlyer]] are optimal for saving on international economy, short domestic options, business class to Europe/Asia, and sweet spots.
