  Virginia EMS Scope of Practice: BLS, ALS, and CCT/Supplemented Transport. Governing docs: 12VAC5-31 (Virginia EMS Regulations), Code of Virginia § 32.1-111.1, EMS Procedures and Medications Schedule, EMS Scope of Practice Formulary/Procedures by OEMS. BLS: FR/EMR/EMT certification, 2-person crew (operator + EMT AIC). ALS: AEMT/Intermediate/Paramedic, invasive procedures + IV meds, 2-3 person crew with ALS AIC + EMT attendant. CCT handled as Supplemented Transport (12VAC5-31-1260): physician/RN/PA as AIC + EMT attendant + operator, requires sending physician determination that care exceeds ALS scope. Neonatal transport: 3 min, RN/PA 2+ yrs or physician. All governed by local protocols, OMD authorization, and EMS Procedures and Medications Schedule.
